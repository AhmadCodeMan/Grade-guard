About This App

Welcome to Grade Guard

Hi, I'm Ahmad Usman, the developer of this app. As a student myself, I understand the importance of keeping track of your academic performance. This app is designed to help you easily calculate your GPA and CGPA with a user-friendly interface and accurate results.

Features:

  • Easy to Use: Simple and intuitive design.
  • Supports Multiple Grading Systems: Fits various educational standards.
  • Real-Time Calculations: Instant results as you input your data.
  • Theme Switching: Toggle between light and dark themes to adapt to different environments and visual preference.

How It Works:

  1. Enter Courses: Add your courses, grades, and credit units.
  2. Calculate: Get your GPA or CGPA instantly.
  3. Review: See detailed breakdowns to understand your performance.
